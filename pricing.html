Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Unused credits carry over once.

Is there a free trial?

Professional and Premium plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Weekly Access does not include a trial period.

Is my data safe for attorney-client privileged material?

The web platform encrypts data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). For firms requiring full data sovereignty, the Enterprise Windows program processes everything locally with zero network dependency.

What are credit add-ons?

Credits let you exceed your plan's included limits without upgrading. Purchase additional PDF processing pages, video minutes, or storage as needed. Credits roll over once (one billing cycle) then expire.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards for web plans. Enterprise Windows accepts credit card, wire transfer, or purchase order.

Do you offer discounts?

Annual billing saves 20%. Founding member rates lock in permanently. Special rates available for public defenders and legal aid organizations.

What does the Enterprise Windows license include?

A perpetual license to run Evident locally on one machine. You supply your own API keys (e.g., OpenAI). No ongoing subscription. Additional seats at $999 each. Optional major version upgrades at 40% of license price.

I'm representing myself (pro se). Which plan?

Weekly Access at $9.99/week is designed for pro se individuals. Pay only for the weeks you need. Add credit packs if you need more processing capacity.
