From Upload to Court-Ready in Minutes

Our streamlined workflow transforms raw evidence into organized, searchable, court-ready exhibits.

1

Upload Your Evidence

Drag and drop BWC videos, police reports, CAD logs, photos, or discovery documents. SHA-256 checksums are generated instantly to preserve chain of custody. Supports MP4, MOV, PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX, and 20+ formats.

2

AI Processes Automatically

Whisper transcribes audio with 99% accuracy. Speaker diarization identifies who said what. OCR extracts text from scanned documents. Entity recognition finds names, dates, and locations. Everything runs locally‚Äîyour data never touches external servers.

3

Search & Cross-Reference

Use natural language queries like "when did the officer arrive?" instead of exact keyword matches. Build interactive timelines, identify contradictions between statements, and cross-reference across all evidence sources.

4

Export Court-Ready Packages

Generate professional exhibits with timestamped transcripts, enhanced media, annotated timelines, and complete chain of custody documentation. Export to PDF, Word, or structured JSON with verification checksums.
