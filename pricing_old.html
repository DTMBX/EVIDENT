Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll pro-rate your billing accordingly.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limits?

You'll be notified when you're approaching your limits. You can either upgrade to a higher tier or wait until the next billing cycle. Enterprise plans have no limits.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for registered non-profit organizations and civil rights groups. Contact sales for details.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start the trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and wire transfer for Enterprise plans.
